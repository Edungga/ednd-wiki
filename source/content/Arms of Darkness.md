You invoke the dark powers. Tendrils of black energy erupt from you and batter all creatures within 10 feet of you. Each creature in that area must make a STR Save. On a failed save, a target takes 2d6 Necrotic damage and can’t take Reactions until its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage, but suffers no other effect.

**At Higher Levels**. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Last remaining bastion of Arcane Knowledge, hunted by the [[Order Arcanum Exterminatus]].

Their members operate in secret, rarely taking on apprentices. Not uncommon to find members near [[The Drowned Archives]]

Once being taken on as an apprentice, one must perform the initiation ritual, which sees them creating their Amber Insignia. This acts as both their badge of membership and arcane focus.

# Hierarchy
There is no centralised ruler, more of a series of classes determined by ones knowledge and arcane prowess

## 1. Archmage
An Archmage is the peak of arcane power, often these mages are often only concerned with one thing; achieving immortality.

Amber Insignia: Amber with complex trim of blackened silver and a mix of other exotic metals

## 2. Master
Once adopting the rank of Master, it is uncommon for them to take on apprentices. They often absorb themselves fully in completing their Arch Thesis.

Amber Insignia: Amber with complex silver trim

One can only ascend to the next rank, archmage, by completing their Arch Thesis and having it acknowledged by other masters or archmages who bestow that rank upon you.

## 3. Mentor
A Magi who takes on an apprentice becomes a Mentor. It is possible for a mage to skip this rank entirely if they never find a suitable apprentice in the time it takes for them to transmute their Amber Insignia with silver.

Amber Insignia: Amber with trims of copper

A Mentor capable of transmuting copper to silver adopts the rank of Master

## 4. Magi
An Apprentice whose mentor has decided knows all their tutelage can pass on becomes a Magi.

Amber Insignia: Amber with trims of Iron

A Magi capable of transmuting iron to copper is eligible to take on an apprentice and adopt the rank of Mentor


## 5. Apprentice
The lowest rank, taken on by a Mentor.

Amber Insignia: Basic Amber

An apprentice capable of transmuting wood to iron is eligible to be granted the rank of Magi by their mentor

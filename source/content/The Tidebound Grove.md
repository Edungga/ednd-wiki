“What the sea takes must one day return.”

The Tidebound Grove is one of the oldest surviving druidic orders, and the most widely known.

Their origins are lost to history, though island legends claim the grove was already ancient when the earliest settlers first arrived upon the Mourning Isles.

The Circle believes the sea is not merely water, but a living memory through which all things eventually pass.

The Circle’s members are often adorned with 
- weather-darkened robes
- driftwood talismans
- whale bone carvings
- shells etched with tidal markings
- cloaks woven from seabird feathers and seal hide

These days the grove plays a heavier hand in the political world with one of their Druids always holding a seat of council within a settlement across [[The Mourning Isles]]

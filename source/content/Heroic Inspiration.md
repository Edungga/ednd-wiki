You can only have 1 Heroic Inspiration at a time. If you gain Heroic Inspiration but already have it, it's lost unless you give it to a player character or [[Retainer]] who lacks it.

You can spend your Heroic Inspiration in one of the following ways:

Reroll d20. Any time you have to Roll a d20, eg for a Save, Check, Death Save, Attack, etc, you may reroll the d20

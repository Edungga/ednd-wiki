Casting Time: Action
Range: 60 ft
Components: S, M (small stones or pebbles)
Duration: Instantaneous
Spell List:
  - Arcane
  - Primal
tags:
  - Spell

You imbue stones with magic causing them to streak toward a creature within range. Make a Ranged Spell Attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 Force damage.

**Cantrip Upgrade**. This spell launches another stone when you reach levels 5 (2 stones), 11 (3 stones), and 17 (4 stones). You can direct the stone at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each stone.

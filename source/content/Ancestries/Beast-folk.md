# Traits
**Creature Type**: Humanoid and Beast

**Size**: Medium (about 4–8 feet tall) or Small (about 2–4 feet tall), chosen when you select this Ancestry

**Walk** Speed: 30 feet

**Life Span**: Varies greatly, some average 40 years, others well into their 300s

**Darkvision**. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

**Bestial Instinct**: You have proficiency in the Perception Skill and can add your Proficiency Bonus to Initiative Rolls.

**Bestial Nature**: You have adopted one of the following Bestial Natures

You make a Melee Weapons Attack with the the weapons you are holding, imbuing it with nature's power. For the duration, you can use your Spellcasting Ability instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of Melee Attacks using that weapon, and the weapon's damage die becomes 1d10. If the attack deals damage, it can be Force damage or the weapon’s normal damage type (your choice).

**Cantrip Upgrade**. This weapon's damage die changes when you reach levels 5 (d12), 11 (2d8), and 17 (2d10).

Music of a style you choose fills the air around you in a 30-foot radius. The music spreads around corners and can be heard from up to 100 feet away. The music moves with you, centred on you for the duration.
Until the spell ends, you make Performance Checks with Advantage. In addition, you can use a Bonus Action to beguile one creature you choose within 30 feet of you that can see you and hear the music. The creature must make a CHA Save. If you or your companions are attacking it, the creature automatically succeeds on the Save. On a failure, the creature becomes friendly to you for as long as it can hear the music and for 1 hour thereafter. You make Deception checks and Persuasion checks against creatures made friendly by this spell with Advantage.

“Nothing crosses the southern pass without tribute.”

Built into the shattered remains of an ancient dwarven gate-fortress at the southern entrance to the Crown of Crows, Troll King’s Rest is one of the most dangerous and heavily contested settlements in the mountains

What was once a proud defensive bastion of the fallen Thanedome has become a brutal scavenger-kingdom ruled by the self-proclaimed Troll King, a massive troll warlord named Malak Long-Tusk.

The Troll King’s forces control many of the surface mines, and probe ever deeper

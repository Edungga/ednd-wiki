Along the storm-lashed western coast stand the remnants of ancient high elven haven once belonging to the Auric Houses, a proud dynasty that ruled trade, diplomacy, and learning, most of their domain now in ruin.

The surviving Auric bloodlines live in isolated cliffside estates and half-ruined coastal courts called the [[City of Gold]]. Though diminished, they maintain elaborate traditions of etiquette, poetry, music, and ceremonial duelling.

To common folk they are:
- impossibly refined,
- emotionally alien,
- and unsettlingly beautiful

Beyond the black waters of the Mirelands lie the Mourning Isles, a ragged chain of wind-beaten islands scattered across the eastern sea. Few ships willingly sail there. Fewer still return unchanged.

The isles are cold, steep, and ancient, crowned by broken cliffs, ruined watchfires, and standing stones older than any kingdom on the mainland. Thin villages cling to narrow coves where gray waves hammer against black rock day and night. The people who live there are hardy, suspicious folk who speak in old dialects and bury their dead beneath cairns facing the sea.

## The End of the World
Beyond the Mourning Isles lies only open ocean and the place sailors call The End of the World. No map agrees on what waits there.

“Where giants fell.”

Barren moorlands littered with standing stones, burial mounds, and the remains of ancient colossi slain in forgotten wars. Primordial energies still seep from the dead titans, warping the land and those who dwell there.

The main populations here, known as the Goliaths, are descendants of people altered over generations by exposure to this power.

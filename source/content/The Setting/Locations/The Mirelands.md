“The drowned frontier.”

A maze of flooded marshes, sunken ruins, forgotten battlefields, and ancient plague pits, all swallowed by the mire.

Permanent settlements are rare and precarious. Most communities survive on stilts, barges, or narrow strips of elevated land.

Travelers in the marshes nail silver coins to boats for luck.

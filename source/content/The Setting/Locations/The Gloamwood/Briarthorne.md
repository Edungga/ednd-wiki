A hidden settlement of the Gloamkin wood elves built among colossal ancient trees.

The settlement is nearly invisible to outsiders unless intentionally revealed. Rope bridges, lanterns, and hidden shrines weave through the upper canopy.

The Gloamkin guard old places within the forest that even they fear.

The Gloamkin are known for:
- moss-dyed cloaks,
- antlered masks worn during rituals,
- guardians of forbidden places
- Feared as forest spirits and witches

A fortified hunting settlement maintained by monster hunters and trappers along the forest edge. The Guild that runs the town is called [[Rangers of the Lodge]], but there are other lesser guilds with sway in town

Its people trade in furs, herbs, monster part, and rare wood.

Few residents willingly enter the deeper forest.

Other Guilds:
- Foragers Guild - who deal mainly in the collection of rare herbs and ingredients
- Woodcutters - who deal in lumber, exotic timbers, and expanding the boarder
- Carvers Guild - who deal in monster bits for various uses

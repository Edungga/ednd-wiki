The Talon is the most widespread criminal syndicate operating within the Mirelands and the surrounding river towns

Unlike the more structured guilds found in the great cities, the Talon is loose, adaptive, and deliberately fragmented, more a shared identity than a single organization.

Its members do not think of themselves as “members” in any formal sense. They are simply Talon-born, people who have learned to survive the drowned world through theft, salvage, and careful violence.

The Talon is divided into semi-independent cells called Nests. 
Each Nest is led by a Claw, who answers to the mythical figurehead of the organisation, an unknown person known as [[The Bird of Prey]]

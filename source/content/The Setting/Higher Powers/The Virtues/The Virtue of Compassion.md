“To understand another is to share their burden.”

Compassion is empathy taken to supernatural extremes. Followers believe isolation and cruelty are the roots of the world’s decay.

This Virtue is often strongest among common folk rather than institutions.
# Followers
- counsellors,
- wandering monks,
- artists,
- and those who ease emotional suffering.
# Symbols
- Joined hands
- Thread and needles
- Shared cups
- Interlocking circles

“Only when all hope is gone do we see the truth.”

Despair is the most feared Virtue.

Its followers believe suffering strips away comforting lies and reveals the world as it truly is. Some become nihilists. Others become terrifyingly pragmatic.

Yet Despair is not necessarily evil. Many who survive unimaginable tragedy find themselves touched by it whether they wish to be or not.
# Followers
- grave keepers,
- survivors of catastrophe,
- hermits,
- failed prophets,
- and those who have seen too much.

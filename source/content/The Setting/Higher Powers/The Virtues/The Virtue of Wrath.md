“There are evils that deserve no mercy.”

Wrath is righteous fury directed toward cruelty, corruption, and injustice. It is not simple anger, it is the terrible certainty that something must be destroyed.

Wrath is dangerous because its followers often begin with noble intentions.
# Followers
- executioners,
- witch hunters,
- revolutionary zealots,
- and those consumed by vengeance.
# Symbols
- Burning swords
- Red hands
- Broken crowns
- Bloodied chains

“Let my suffering spare another.”

Sacrifice is devotion through suffering. It teaches that true goodness demands personal cost.

Its followers are both admired and feared, because they often willingly endure terrible burdens.
# Followers
- martyrs,
- guardians,
- plague tenders,
- penitents,
- and wandering protectors.
# Symbols
- Thorn crowns
- Bound hands
- Blood on white cloth
- Broken blades

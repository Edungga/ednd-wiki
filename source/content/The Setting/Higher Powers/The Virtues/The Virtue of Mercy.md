“No soul is beyond compassion.”

Mercy embodies forgiveness, restraint, and the refusal to abandon another person to suffering.

Its followers believe cruelty is easy, but mercy requires true strength.

Mercy is deeply respected in a brutal world, though many see its followers as tragically soft-hearted.
# Followers
- battlefield medics,
- caretakers,
- judges,
- and those seeking redemption.
# Symbols
- Open hands
- White cloth
- Flowing water
- Unbroken chains

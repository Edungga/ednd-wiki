
# The Virtues
The Virtues are not gods in the old sense.

They have no creation myths, no celestial realms, and no agreed upon forms. They do not answer prayers with voices from the heavens, nor do they demand worship in the manner of kings or tyrants.

The Virtues are understood as vast unseen wills bound to powerful truths within the human soul. Devotees to the Virtues do not necessarily gain power through worship alone. Their magic often manifests through conviction, sacrifice, obsession, or emotional extremity.

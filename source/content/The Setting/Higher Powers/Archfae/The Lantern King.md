*The Lord of Lost Roads*

The Lantern King holds power over crossroads, lonely paths, wandering souls, and travellers lost in the fog.

He appears as a tall figure carrying an antler lantern filled with stars instead of flame. His face is never visible beneath his weather-worn cloak.

It is said those who become hopelessly lost sometimes encounter him standing silently beside the road. Those who follow his lantern are always led somewhere, though not necessarily where they intended to go.

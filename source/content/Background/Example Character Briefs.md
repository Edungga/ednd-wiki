- **Skill Proficiencies**: Religion, Medicine

- **Feat**. [[Feat#Healer|Healer]]
- **Stats**. 16, 8, 13, 8, 13, 16
- **Class**. [[Paladin]]
	- Skills. Athletics, Insight
	- Spells. [[Command]], [[Cure Wounds]], [[Shield of Faith]], [Wrathful Smite]
